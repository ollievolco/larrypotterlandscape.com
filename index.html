About Us

Larry Potter Landscape Services is a small start up business. Larry has over 45 years of experience in the fields of horticulture, landscape design, and irrigation. Larry started his career working for the family business in commercial spray application in the 1960's. His major course work in high school was horticulture which he carried on into college. Larry went to work for Portland Parks and Recreation, where he stayed for 16 years, he achieved the title of Athletic Field Maintenance Supervisor. He then went on to become the Park Operations and Facility Manager for Oregon City Parks for the next 13 years. After retiring from Oregon City Parks he is pursuing his passion for landscaping and water conservation
